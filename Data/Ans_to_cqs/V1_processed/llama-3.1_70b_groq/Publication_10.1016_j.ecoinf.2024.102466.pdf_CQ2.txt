The data format used in the deep learning pipeline is images. The study mentions that the dataset comprises 9717 images of 15 wild bird species acquired by camera traps in China's Northeast Tiger and Leopard National Park. The images were used to train and evaluate the performance of 8 deep learning algorithms for bird object detection. There is no mention of audio or video data formats being used in the study. Additionally, CSV (Comma Separated Values) is not mentioned as a data format used in the deep learning pipeline.
