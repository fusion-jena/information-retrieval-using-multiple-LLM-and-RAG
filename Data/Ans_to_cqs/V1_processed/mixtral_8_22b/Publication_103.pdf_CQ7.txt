The data was split based on the proportion of the data provided by the PC challenge. For Herbarium255, the data was divided into 70% training data and the remaining 30% as test data. This division was done for computing the top-1 and top-5 classification accuracy. Similarly, Herbarium1K was divided into 80% training data and 20% testing data. The separation was carried out by species, and within each species, the division was made accordingly.
