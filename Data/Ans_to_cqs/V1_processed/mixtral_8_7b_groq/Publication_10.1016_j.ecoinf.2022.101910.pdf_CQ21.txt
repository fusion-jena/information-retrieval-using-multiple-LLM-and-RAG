Metrics calculation typically involves determining the performance of the model on a validation or test dataset. This can include measures such as accuracy, precision, recall, and F1 score. Accuracy is the proportion of correct predictions out of all predictions made. Precision is the proportion of true positive predictions (correctly identifying a bird vocalization) out of all positive predictions. Recall is the proportion of true positive predictions out of all actual positive instances in the data. The F1 score is the harmonic mean of precision and recall, providing a balance between the two measures.
