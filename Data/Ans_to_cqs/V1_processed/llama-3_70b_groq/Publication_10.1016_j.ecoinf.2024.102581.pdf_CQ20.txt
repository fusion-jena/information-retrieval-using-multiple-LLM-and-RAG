The hardware resources used for training the deep learning model are an NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2080 GPU with 8 GB of GDDR6 VRAM and 2944 CUDA cores. This GPU accelerated neural network training and inference. Additionally, the workstation is equipped with an Intel i7 CPU operating at a base clock speed of 3.80 GHz and a boost clock speed of 4.12 GHz, 16 GB of high-speed DDR4 RAM running at 3200 MHz, and a 1 TB NVMe SSD (Samsung 970 EVO Plus) as the primary drive for the operating system and software installation.
