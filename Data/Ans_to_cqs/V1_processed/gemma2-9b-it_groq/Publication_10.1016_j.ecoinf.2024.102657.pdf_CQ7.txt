The data is split into three sets: training, validation, and testing. 

* **Training set:** This set, comprising 70% of the songs in each dialect class, is used to train the CNN spectrogram classifier and the k-NN location classifier. 

* **Validation set:** This set, comprising 10% of the songs in each dialect class, is used to tune the hyperparameters of the CNN.

* **Testing set:** This set, comprising 20% of the songs in each dialect class, is held out and used to evaluate the performance of the trained models.
