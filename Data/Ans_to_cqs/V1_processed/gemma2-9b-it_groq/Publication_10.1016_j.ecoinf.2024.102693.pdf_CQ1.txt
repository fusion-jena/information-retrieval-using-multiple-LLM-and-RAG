To answer your query, here are some common methods for collecting raw data used in deep learning:

* **Surveys:**  Used for gathering structured information from individuals, often through online questionnaires.
* **Sensors:**  Physical devices that collect data from the environment, such as temperature, pressure, location, or images.
* **Public Datasets:**  Collections of data made available by organizations or researchers for use by the general public. These can cover a wide range of domains, including images, text, audio, and numerical data.
* **Web Scraping:**  Automatically extracting data from websites.
* **APIs:**  Application Programming Interfaces allow developers to access and retrieve data from various sources, such as social media platforms or financial markets.
