The purpose of the deep learning model mentioned in the context is to downscale near-surface wind fields to high-resolution topography in highly complex terrain. This is stated in the paper "Wind-topo: downscaling near-surface wind fields to high-resolution topography in highly complex terrain with deep learning" by Dujardin and Lehning (2022). The model is used to predict wind fields at a high resolution, taking into account the complex topography of the terrain.
