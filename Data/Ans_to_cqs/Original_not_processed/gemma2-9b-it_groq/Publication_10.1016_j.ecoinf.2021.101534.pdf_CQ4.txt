The provided text describes several data augmentation techniques specific to audio data, particularly spectrograms. These include:

* **Frequency, pitch, and volume changes:**  Altering these aspects of the audio signal.
* **Time and frequency domain transformations:** Shifting, rotating, and zooming spectrograms in both the time and frequency domains.
* **Noise addition:**  Introducing various types of noise to the audio data.
* **Sound mixing:** Combining the target signal with other sounds or reverberations.
* **Mosaic augmentation:** Merging four spectrograms into one larger image, randomly placing sound objects within.
* **Rainbow mapping:** Encoding frequency information into a spectrogram using a gradient colormap for each frequency band.
