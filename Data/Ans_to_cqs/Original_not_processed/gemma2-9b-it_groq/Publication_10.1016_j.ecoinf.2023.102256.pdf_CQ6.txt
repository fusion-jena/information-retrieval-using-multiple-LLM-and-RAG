However, common preprocessing steps for various types of data include:

* **Normalization:** Scaling features to a specific range (e.g., 0 to 1) to prevent features with larger magnitudes from dominating the learning process.
* **Standardization:** Transforming data to have zero mean and unit variance. This can improve the performance of some algorithms.
* **Cleaning:** Handling missing values, removing outliers, and correcting inconsistencies in the data.
* **Encoding:** Converting categorical variables into numerical representations that the model can understand (e.g., one-hot encoding).

The specific preprocessing steps will depend on the nature of the data and the chosen deep learning model.
