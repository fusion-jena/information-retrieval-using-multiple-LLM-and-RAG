The text describes the following data augmentation techniques used in the deep learning pipeline:

* **Horizontal flip:**  The image is flipped horizontally.
* **Grayscale:** The image is converted to grayscale.
* **Blur:** The image is blurred up to 10 pixels.
* **Noise:**  Up to 10% of the pixels in the image are randomly assigned noise.
* **Cropping:** A patch with the same size as the original image is randomly cropped from the image.
