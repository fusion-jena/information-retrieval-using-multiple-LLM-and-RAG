The text provides the following metrics for evaluating the deep learning model's performance:

* **Overall Accuracy (OA):** The ratio of correctly classified pixels to the total number of pixels in the test dataset.
* **Producer Accuracy (PA):**  Accuracy from the perspective of the mapmaker.
* **User Accuracy (UA):** The probability that a pixel classified in a category actually belongs to that category on the ground.
* **Kappa coefficient of agreement:**  A measure of inter-rater reliability for categorical items.
