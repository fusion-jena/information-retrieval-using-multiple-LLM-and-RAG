The text describes three data augmentation techniques applied to the delphinid tonal and biological click classes:

* **Pitch shift:**  Altering the pitch of the audio signal by a random amount between 0.5 and 1.5.
* **Time-shift:** Shifting the audio signal in time by a random amount between -300 ms and 300 ms.
* **Added noise:**  Adding Gaussian white noise to the audio signal with a power between 1 and 2.
