The provided text mentions data augmentation for deep learning but doesn't list specific techniques.  

However, based on common knowledge, some widely used data augmentation techniques include:

* **Flipping:** Horizontally or vertically flipping images.
* **Rotating:** Rotating images by a certain angle.
* **Scaling:**  Enlarging or shrinking images by a specific percentage.
* **Translation:**  Shifting images horizontally or vertically.
* **Color Jitter:**  Adjusting brightness, contrast, saturation, and hue.
* **Noise Injection:** Adding random noise to images. 


You can find a more comprehensive list and explanations in the Shorten and Khoshgoftaar paper referenced in the context.
