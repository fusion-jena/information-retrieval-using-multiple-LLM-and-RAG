The purpose of the deep learning model, specifically MegaDetector, is object detection. It is designed to detect and classify objects in images, particularly in the context of wildlife camera trap images. The model uses a two-step process, first identifying regions in an image that contain an object and then examining the attributes of those regions to assign objects to a particular class. The model can detect and classify objects as either an animal, human, or truck, and provides a confidence value indicating the model's confidence in the selected object class.
