The data repository link associated with the deep learning pipeline can be found on Zenodo. The dataset, which includes analyzed citizen science and social media data with associated metadata, is available at <https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.8348734>. This data was collected in 2023 by ASC (email: [sofia.cardoso@cibio.up.pt](mailto:sofia.cardoso@cibio.up.pt)) and consists of 3274 images.

References:
