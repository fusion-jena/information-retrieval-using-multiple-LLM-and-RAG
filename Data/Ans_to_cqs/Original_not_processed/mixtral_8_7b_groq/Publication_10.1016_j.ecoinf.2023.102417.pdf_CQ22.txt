Classification accuracy gives a ratio of the correct classifications to all classifications, describing the percentage of correct classifications. However, for unbalanced datasets, this measure is not very significant. To overcome this issue, the Intersection over Union (IoU) is used. The IoU is calculated as the intersection of two sets of classified quantities (A ∩ B) divided by the union of these quantities (A ∪ B).
